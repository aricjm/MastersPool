Rules

Follow the steps below:

  • Cost

    • Each entry cost $5
    • No max on number of entries per person
    • If entry(s) are not paid before April 7th, they will be removed
    • Pay using Venmo or in person to Aric.

  • How to play

    • The golfers are ranked into 6 tiers
    • Pick one golfer from each tier
    • Each day your score will be the sum of your top four performing golfers, no matter the tier
    • If, after the cut, three or more of your golfers did not make the cut, they will be given the same score as the person in last
    • At the end of the tournament the entry with the lowest total score wins
    • In event of a tie, the other two golfers scores (combined) will decide the winner
